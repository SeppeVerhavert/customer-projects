Responding to Daily Wood Stressors

When dealing with periods of heightened stress in the process, Advanced Woods require different skills from those used for building long-term relationships. .

Faced with an intense, difficult, or heated situation, Advanced Woods will not have an easy time Identifying the disruptive thoughts that are feeding their reactive state or coming up with creative ways to solve what they likely think is a downright attack. At the peak of a Wood’s alienation, they shut out the possibility of calmly sitting down and talking through their challenges. .

In a Wood’s troublesome moments, such as when anger and frustration peak, Fire serves as Wood’s immediate release valve. Fire wisdom will most likely help Advanced Woods reconnect and recover from a stress trigger. .

Here are some mental and physical Fire Emergency Relief tools to release the pressure created by Advanced Wood stressors: .

Change Your Wood Mental State

.

• Identify one to three of the disruptive thoughts that are coming up for you in the stressful moment. Assess on a scale of one to five how intense your anger is, one being least severe and five being most intense.
• Look around your office and name five things you like or that make you happy. This approach helps you stay in the present instead of losing yourself in a rapidly escalating frenzy about your situation.
• Look at reminders of your successes. These could be certificates, trophies, emails, handwritten thank-you notes, photos, or diplomas.
• Speak to a coworker or friend you can count on to be optimistic and encouraging in the face of pessimism.

Change Your Wood Physical State

.

• Squeeze an object like a stress ball that helps you release the tension building up at the moment.
• Practice hamstrings, glutes, quadriceps, back, and chest stretches to relieve built-up tension.

If Advanced Woods are too frustrated to connect to their Fire abilities, they should move their bodies to blow off some steam first. Go for a run, take a brisk walk, toss a ball against a wall, or simply leave the room for a moment.

Once over the initial hurdle of disorderly thinking, when a sense of calm begins to reappear, Advanced Woods then have an easier connection to the skills from your other four Practical Wisdom types that will help expand your ability to solve the challenge that kicked up stress in the first place.

Integrated Wood Wisdom

I don’t want to leave you hanging, wondering when and how you’ll know your Advanced Wood wisdom is highly integrated with your other four Practical Wisdom types.

Wisdom is not the absence of stress and challenge in your life. For Advanced Woods, it’s about being able to navigate the challenging moments of your life while paying attention to and supporting those around you who’s Wood skills may be low and in need of a boost. At its core, Practical Wisdom allows you to nimbly work with the people in your life for the betterment of you, the world in which you live.

As your ability to maintain solid connections in the face of stressful situations expands, you will be less triggered by other people’s opinions and tendencies. You will also become less likely to attach to relationships that don’t serve you well or in which your needs for stable connections are not being met. When you calibrate and integrate your Advanced Wisdom type, you will be fully equipped to form equally strong connections with other Advanced type people. This is because you will clearly see the valuable skills, they each bring to your relationships.

When you, as an Advanced Wood, are well connected to your other four Practical Wisdom types, you’re likely to skillfully manage many, if not all, of the relationships between:
