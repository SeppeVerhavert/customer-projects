Purpose

The purpose of this report is to help you reflect on your strengths and development areas related to your work as a learning specialist, and when considered in conjunction with your school's strategic priorities, assist you in developing a targeted and impactful professional development plan.


What's in this report?

1. Your overall results

2. Your results for each of the six capability areas, including the overall result for that area and an item level analysis.

3. Development resources to help you further build your knowledge and skills.


Scoring

Item scoring

Each item/question is given a score from 1 - 4, based on the response that you selected for that item. For example, if you selected the level 1 response, your score will be 1 for that item.

Overall result

Your overall result for each capability area is calculated from your average score for that capability area. The scoring bands are as follows:

Level Average score
Level 1: Emerging 1 - 1.4
Level 2: Evolving 1.5 - 2.4
Level 3: Embedding 2.5 - 3.4
Level 4: Excelling 3.5 - 4

The highest average score you can achieve for any capability area is 4, which indicates that you have selected Level 4 responses for every item in that capability area.
