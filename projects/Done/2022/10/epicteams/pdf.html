Is your team storming, norming, or performing?

High performing teams don’t happen overnight. The typically evolve over time and experience four necessary stages of development. Those stages are forming, storming, norming, and performing. Together, they make up what is called the Team Maturity Curve.

Definition of a High Performing Team: A team that (1) meets the needs of the people they serve and (2) continuously learns and grows together over time

You can greatly accelerate the time it takes to become a high performing team by using the following report as a starting point. This report is intended to be a “mirror” for your team to reflect on which behaviors are supporting its evolution and which ones hindering its progress.
